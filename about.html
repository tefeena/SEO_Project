Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bot-It?

Bot-It is an online platform designed to automate repetitive tasks. Whether you want to schedule posts, book reservations, or manage your online business, Bot-It simplifies these processes and saves you time.

How Does Bot-It Work?

Bot-It uses a combination of algorithms and pre-programmed bots to carry out specific tasks. Once you set up your bot, it will automatically complete tasks for you without the need for constant oversight.

Is Bot-It Secure?

Yes, Bot-It takes security very seriously. All data transfers are encrypted, and we follow industry-standard security protocols to ensure that your information is safe.

Do I Need Coding Skills to Use Bot-It?

No! Bot-It is designed to be user-friendly, and you do not need any coding skills to get started. Our drag-and-drop interface makes setting up bots simple and fast.

What Happens if I Outgrow My Plan?

If you find that your automation needs are growing, you can easily upgrade to a higher plan that offers more features and greater flexibility.
